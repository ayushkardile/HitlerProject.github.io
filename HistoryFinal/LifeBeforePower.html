  • Hitler origins

    • In a small town called "Brauna Am Inn" one of the most horrific and feared periods was born (1889 - Hitler was born). Unlike most of the other dictators and monarchies of the time, Hitler was not born into power. Hitler started with an average simple life he had a normal education and as a young man, he showed and possessed no special talents. Hitler's Mom died when he was 17, this caused him to move to Vienna in hopes of pursuing his love of Art in an art school but he was rejected because of his lack of talent and creativity.

  • Years of War
    • Although born an Austrian Some time after his failed art endeavors Hitler volunteered to join the German army. He was assigned to the ground troops as a courier although this was a dangerous job it was not as dangerous as the soldiers fighting on the frontline. In December 1914 Hitler received the Iron Cross for his bravery. In a letter to his landlord, he said that it was the best day of his life. In April 1918 Hitler and his regiment were attacked and half of the soldiers were killed. Hitler did not survive and received another Iron Cross. When Germany lost the war in November 1918 Hitler was in a military hospital because his eyes were hurt due to poison gas attacks. When he heard about the German surrender he fell into a mental crisis, Since his war experiences had a big influence on his thinking.
